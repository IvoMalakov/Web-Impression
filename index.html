Our Vault

We store our diamonds in 5 Tax-Free Zones, where they’re audited by IDEX, and regulated by local authorities. The diamDEXX platform then connects them to 7,000 different diamond manufacturers offering 800,000 fully-certified stones.

Through this ecosystem, we facilitate a system where diamonds are used as a collateral to make DiamCoin perfectly liquid. Our users then become free from external, systematic, or internal restrictions, allowing them to access financial freedom with ease

One DiamCoin will always equal 1 US Dollar through our dynamic smart-vault system, and users will always be able to convert their DIAM back to USD. DiamCoin will also be used as a payment method both on our platform and our partners’, while accepting no intermediaries. DiamCoin will always be transacted from user to user only

Price Consistency in a Nutshell

Thanks to our smart-vault system, we are able to keep 1 DIAM at $1. To do it, we’ve designed a smart-contract that automatically adjusts the number of diamonds in our vaults in the following situations:

-The market price of diamonds goes up, or
-The market price of diamonds goes down.

When diamonds’ price goes up, we will adjust our vaults by reducing the number of diamonds on them, and/or issuing more tokens.

And, when/if the price decreases, we will burn tokens and/or buy new diamonds to ensure our currency’s stability.

About us

Established in 2010, Hello Group’s headquarters were located in Cyprus. Hello Group started as provider of premium custom-made white label front & back end solutions. Our partners benefited from comprehensive technological solutions, delivered in few weeks and fully supported by our international team of experts. Hello Diamonds was created few years later, in 2015, with the idea of taking the existing success of the diamond industry and adapting it to our expertise, therefore raising its efficiency and overall accessibility. DiamDEXX, the blockchain solution of Hello Diamonds, has now its headquarter in Canada, and operational offices in Limassol, offices in Antwerp and New York.

As software and CRM experts, Hello Group has created Hello Diamonds with the idea of sourcing the diamond industry’s best precious stones into a comprehensive and user-friendly web platform. This gives access to over 800,000 live diamonds in one single portal, allowing users to easily navigate through the extensive list of precious stones and filtering them by a multitude of factors including cut, carat, colour, and value.

The Hello Diamonds team created a platform that is accessible to all users, and is easily navigable, offering any individual the ability to purchase and resell diamonds in just a few seconds. Certificates of authenticity can be downloaded and physically delivered, and you can keep track of all your transactions — all in one platform.

Then the idea of blockchain and security started to become more and more important and the idea behind Hello Diamonds became really simple: rather than investing in highly volatile assets such as stocks, bonds or commodities, or in highly volatile cryptocurrencies or depreciating fiat-backed crypto, some investors are more likely to trust the long lasting, trustworthy value of diamonds. After months of development, we have come with DiamCoin, the first 100% token backed by physical diamonds.
